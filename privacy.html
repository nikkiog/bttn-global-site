This policy covers only information handled through this website. It does not cover:

03Information we collect

This is a simple informational website. We aim to collect as little as possible:

  • Information you send us. If you email us at the address above, we receive your email address and whatever you choose to include in your message.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for advertising.

We use the limited information described above only to:

We do not sell or rent personal information. We may share limited information with service providers who help us operate the website (for example, our hosting provider), or where required by law. Any such providers are expected to handle information consistent with this policy.

06Data retention

We keep information only as long as needed for the purposes described here or as required by law. Email correspondence is retained as long as reasonably necessary to address your inquiry and our records.

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you, or to object to certain processing. To make a request, email us at info@baileyttnglobal.com and we will respond as required by applicable law. Note that requests relating to a specific product should be directed to that product’s operator through its own website.

08Children’s privacy

This website is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them through this site.

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to info@baileyttnglobal.com, or by mail to BTTN Global, Miami, FL, USA.
